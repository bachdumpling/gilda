Orders & Shipping

Gilda products are designed to last. If you have any questions about the product or fit, you can send an email to info@gildaclothing.com.

We offer free shipping on all orders within the US.

Orders will be processed Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. Orders will be shipped within 48 hours of being received. You will receive confirmation by email when goods are shipped.

Returns

As a small, independent label, Gilda does not currently accept returns or refunds. If you'd like to exchange for a different size, please email us.

Please review your order carefully before completing your purchase.

If you live in the NY area, you can try on the product at C'H'C'M' on Bond St.
